About Us

Learn About Us

Best School For Your Kids

Welcome to Purvaansh Preschool, where we embrace a fresh and innovative approach to early childhood education. Our mission is to provide a nurturing and engaging environment that fosters a love for learning in every child.

Our Philosophy:
At our core, we champion experiential learning and play, merging traditional methods with modern approaches for a comprehensive educational journey tailored to each child's unique needs.

What We Offer

Holistic Development

We prioritize play-based learning to foster creativity, critical thinking, and social skills development.

Flexible Programs

We provide full-day, half-day, and extended care options to suit busy families' needs.

Safe Environment

Our secure, child-friendly facilities enable children to explore, learn, and grow confidently.

Compact Curriculum

Our curriculum covers language, math, science, arts, and physical education, making learning enjoyable and meaningful.

Holistic Development

We balance academics with activities like music, dance, and mindfulness for overall development.

Community Involvement

We host regular parent-teacher meetings, family events, and workshops to build a strong community.

Our Daily Routine

Morning Circle Time

A welcoming session where children greet each other, share news, and discuss the day's activities.

Story Time and Rest

A quiet story time followed by a rest period to recharge after lunch allows for rejuvenation.

Outdoor Play

A safe playground where children can engage in physical activities and enjoy fresh air.

Thematic Learning Centers

Engaging centers for reading, math, science, and drawing throughout the day.

Afternoon Activities

Thematic learning and hands-on projects to end the day with creativity and exploration.

Snack Time

Incorporate a designated time for a nutritious snack to keep children energized throughout the day.
